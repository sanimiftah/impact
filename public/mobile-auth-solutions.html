🛠️ Quick Implementation Guide

Recommended: Option 2 - Bottom Slide Bar

Add this HTML after the floating auth widget:

<!-- Mobile Bottom Bar Auth -->
<div id="mobileAuthBar" class="mobile-auth-bar">
  <div class="flex items-center justify-between">
    <span class="text-sm text-gray-700">👤 Guest Mode</span>
    <button onclick="showAuthModal()" class="bg-green-500 text-white px-4 py-2 rounded-lg">
      Sign In
    </button>
  </div>
</div>

Alternative: Option 3 - Top Banner

Add this before the navigation:

<!-- Mobile Top Banner Auth -->
<div id="mobileTopBanner" class="mobile-top-banner">
  <div class="flex items-center justify-between">
    <span class="text-sm">👤 Welcome to IMPACT!</span>
    <button onclick="showAuthModal()" class="bg-white text-green-600 px-3 py-1 rounded">
      Sign In
    </button>
  </div>
</div>
